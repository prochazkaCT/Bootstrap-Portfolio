About Me

Hello and welcome -- you have landed on my portfolio -- a work in progress for sure! I strive for a balanced life in Chapel Hill, NC with my husband, two kids (10 & 8) and our wacky senior dog, Rizoe (nearing 16!).

Somehow I have managed to work for a handful of fortune 500 companies, including Enron, Intel, and GE. I gave up the semi-conductor world to pursue a Master’s of Science in Environmental Toxicology and squeezed in a Geographical Information Systems minor (GIS). Being a visual person, mapping data was intuitive.

Check out my LinkedIn page for an in-depth look at what I have been up to over the years.
